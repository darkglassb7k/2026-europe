## Style/tone guidelines learned

- **Language:** all English, casual travel-influencer voice (not literal translation, not stiff)
- **Punctuation:** no em/en dashes in prose (titles OK); use `·`, commas, periods
- **Slot items:** clean labels only, no descriptive fluff ("Pisa" not "Half-day in Pisa · do the leaning tower thing")
- **Card titles/day-tags:** can retain personality ("Wheels Up · TPE", "Windmills & a Community Garden", "Dawn train · beating the crowds")
- **Emoji:** removed everywhere; replaced with Lucide icons
- **Chinese:** removed from user-facing text; site is fully English
